Location

Stay in the heart of London

With views of London’s skyline, DoubleTree by Hilton Hotel London features a rooftop bar Savage Garden, and offers air-conditioned and soundproofed rooms. The Tower of London and Tower Bridge are just a 5-minute walk away.

Featuring floor-to-ceiling windows and luxury décor, standard rooms have 49-inch flat-screen TVs, while executive rooms and suites have 55-inch flat-screen TVs. Rooms also have an en suite bathroom with a mist-free mirror, a power shower and designer toiletries.

Just 150 m from Tower Hill Underground Station, DoubleTree by Hilton Tower of London is a 5-minute walk from the iconic Gherkin and The City. With 8 flexible event spaces, the hotel is able to cater for special occasions or celebrations.
